
# Skynet

### From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Jump to: navigation, search

Skynet may refer to:

  * Skynet (satellites), a UK military communications system
  * Skynet (Terminator), the fictional computer network in the Terminator series of films
  * SkyNET, a PC game based on the Terminator film series

Skynet may also refer to a company:

  * Skynet Nuuk, a wireless network covering the capital of Greenland
  * Belgacom Skynet, a Belgian interactive digital media company
  * Loral Skynet or Telesat, a Canadian satellite communications
  * Skynet Asia Airways, a low-cost Japanese airline
  * Skynet Airlines, a defunct Irish airline
